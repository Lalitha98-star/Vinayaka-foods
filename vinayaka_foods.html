👨‍🍳 Cooking Directions (Dosa)

1
Take the ready wet mix into a bowl and add salt as required.
2
Spread dosa mix evenly on the pan with medium flame heat.
3
Sprinkle little oil on dosa and cook till golden brown.
4
Now serve hot dosa with chutney or masala curry. Enjoy!
